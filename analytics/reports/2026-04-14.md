## Actions Taken

### 1. Mid-Page Inline CTA Component (HIGH IMPACT — big bet of the session)
**Why:** 12 sessions of SEO growth (306 impressions, 37 pages) with 0 signups. Root cause analysis: the only CTA on every SEO page is at the very bottom, after all content + related guides + cross-category links. Users who bounce (likely majority) never see it.

**Solution:** Created `InlineCta` component — a visually distinct, mid-page CTA with:
- Primary-tinted background card (border-primary/20, bg-primary/5)
- Zap icon + variant-specific heading
- Variant-specific description explaining what WadeCV does for their use case
- 3 trust bullets: "1 free credit on signup", "AI-powered fit analysis", "ATS-optimised formatting"
- Large CTA button: "Try it free — no credit card needed"
- Tracked via existing `trackSeoCtaClick()` analytics

**Placement:** After body content section, before FAQ (mid-page). This ensures users see a CTA after reading the most valuable content, even if they don't scroll to the bottom.

**Files created:**
- `frontend/src/components/seo/inline-cta.tsx` — Client component with 7 variant configs

**Files changed (import + component placement):**
- `frontend/src/app/(resources)/skills/[slug]/page.tsx`
- `frontend/src/app/(resources)/jobs/[slug]/page.tsx`
- `frontend/src/app/(resources)/resume-bullets/[slug]/page.tsx`
- `frontend/src/app/(resources)/ats/[slug]/page.tsx`
- `frontend/src/app/(resources)/career-change/[slug]/page.tsx`
- `frontend/src/app/(resources)/physical-mail/[slug]/page.tsx`
- `frontend/src/app/(resources)/company-resume/[company]/page.tsx`

**Affects:** All 170+ SEO content detail pages now have TWO CTAs — one mid-page (inline) and one at bottom (original Card CTA). The mid-page CTA is the one users will actually see.

**Waiting for:** Track `seo_cta_click` events in GA4 to measure impact. First conversions should appear within days if traffic starts clicking through.

## Strategic Notes

1. **Conversion is now the #1 priority.** We've built the top-of-funnel machine (306 impressions, growing). The bottleneck is converting impressions → clicks → visits → signups. The inline CTA addresses the visit → signup gap, but we're still gated on position improvement for the impressions → clicks gap.

2. **Growth is decelerating:** +4% this session vs +8% last session. This is expected — the initial burst from new content is normalizing. The next acceleration will come from: (a) comparison pages getting indexed, (b) positions improving from 70-90 to 30-50 range, (c) new content categories.

3. **Mobile is growing fast:** 14 impressions (+40%), 7.14% CTR vs 0.35% desktop CTR. Mobile users who find us are clicking. This validates the need for mobile-friendly CTAs (InlineCta is responsive).

4. **International spread accelerating:** 20 countries now showing impressions. Germany, Hong Kong, South Korea, Mozambique are new. The UK market (27%) continues to grow.

5. **Python-developer cluster completion validated:** Position improved to 59.0 (from 71.7 when first tracked). Cluster strategy works.
