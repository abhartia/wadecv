- Teal, Jobscan, Wobo AI, ChatGPT review sections now link to their `/wadecv-vs-*` pages

- Each comparison page now links to 3-4 other comparison pages via CrossCategoryLinks contextLinks

**Expected impact:** Google should now discover and index comparison pages within 3-5 days through internal link crawling.
