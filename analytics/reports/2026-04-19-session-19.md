## Strategic Notes

- **Why this beats writing another comparison page:** every comparison page we
  shipped (Teal, Jobscan, Zety, FlowCV, Wobo, Rezi, Claude, Enhancv, AiApply,
  JobCopilot) targets a specific competitor brand. The ATS Resume Checker page
  targets a product category — "ats resume checker" is a generic transactional
  query with much higher aggregate volume than any single competitor brand.
- **9 new direct internal links to `/ats/[slug]` pages.** This reinforces the
  `/ats` cluster at the exact moment `/ats/lever` is stuck at position 8 with
  0 clicks. If users enter the funnel via the checker page, they may also click
  through to the platform-specific guides.
- **The checker page is a conversion surface, not a content surface.** Every
  section pushes toward "sign up, use 1 free credit, get a score". The
  comparison table is honest (it admits Jobscan's free tier exists) but
  emphasises that WadeCV bundles the rewrite for free — a real differentiator.
- **UK coverage included.** FAQ #10 explicitly handles UK CV scenarios and
  links to `/free-cv-builder-uk`, so the 28%-of-traffic UK segment isn't lost.
- **SoftwareApplication schema with rating** should help rich-result eligibility
  and may produce a rating snippet on SERPs — watch for this in GSC around
  2026-04-26+.
