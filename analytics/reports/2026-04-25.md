## Big bet of the session: `/customer-service-resume` pillar + cluster

### Why customer service

1. **Largest impression cluster on site.** 91 impressions on `/skills/customer-service` alone, ~46 across 24 different customer-service queries — every other vertical pales. The S22 worklog explicitly queued: "if customer-service cluster doesn't lift by S26 from S22 freshness, run pillar → /skills/customer-service internal-link density experiment (not more content)". S25 = 1 session early, but freshness-only has plateaued (impressions grew but position unchanged at 80s) — pillar is the right move.
2. **Pillar+cluster playbook validated.** S20's `/consulting-resume` drove 53.8 → 23.7 daily-avg position lift in 7 days (confirmed in S22). S21's `/investment-banking-resume` is in patience window. Customer service is the third application of the same template.
3. **Two thin underlying pages found.** `/jobs/customer-service-representative` had body 491 chars / 1 FAQ / 3 commonMistakes / broken `salaryRange` (currency-strip bug — same one S22/S23 fixed on admin/accountant). `/resume-bullets/customer-service` had body 460 chars / 5 bullets / 0 FAQs. Both are exact candidates for the same overhaul template.
4. **CSM-track separation is a 2026 trend not yet covered.** NRR / GRR / logo churn / book-size-in-$ARR is the screening stack for customer success in 2026 vs the CSAT / FCR / AHT stack for support. Pillar explicitly differentiates these tracks.
