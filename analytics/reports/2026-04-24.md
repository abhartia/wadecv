## Strategic Notes

1. **Trend-data pipeline has been silently broken for 3 sessions.** I accepted "429 rate-limited" from the error messages without reading the full exception. The real message was `Retry.__init__() got an unexpected keyword argument 'method_whitelist'` — a `TypeError` from urllib3's retry subclass, not an HTTP response. The diagnosis lesson: when a pull script "rate-limits" three consecutive days on different IPs / UAs, that is evidence against rate-limiting and in favour of a library-version incompat. Future agents should read the full `except Exception as e` body, not pattern-match for "429" in the string.

2. **Comparison-page playbook has mature repeatability.** The pattern is now: (a) identify +X00% rising competitor signal in Trends; (b) create `/wadecv-vs-{slug}` using the `/wadecv-vs-resumeai` template; (c) add to sitemap at 0.9, to `TOOL_COMPARISONS`, to listicle as new numbered entry with deep-link. One session per competitor. Resume.io is the 13th competitor this template has been applied to; the playbook now ships in ~90 minutes with low risk of regression.

3. **The compression curve is running ahead of schedule.** Four weeks ago the site had ~10 impressions/day at pos 60+. Today's 2026-04-22 point is 5 impressions at pos 10.2. Even accounting for single-day noise, that's a ~6x position improvement over a month. If this continues, the next structural move is a third pillar — possibly `/product-management-resume` or `/software-engineer-resume` — targeting the next high-LTV vertical.

4. **Resume.io differs from prior comparisons in how it's positioned.** Unlike Jobscan (tactical analyzer, direct alternative) or Teal (application tracker, different product), Resume.io is a direct template-builder competitor — same "start from blank page" workflow that Zety, FlowCV, Kickresume, Novoresume, Enhancv, and Resume.io all share.
